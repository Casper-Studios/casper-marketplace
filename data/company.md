# Casper Studios

## About

Casper Studios is a full-service AI product studio that designs and builds custom AI solutions. We help organizations—from startups and creators to hedge funds, public companies, and government institutions—leverage AI to solve complex challenges.

Founded in 2022 and headquartered in Union City, CA, we're a profitable, bootstrapped company focused on sustainable growth over unicorn status.
